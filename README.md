## 📌 Project Description

TrendAura is an AI-powered digital wardrobe and fashion assistant designed to help users manage their clothing, plan outfits, and stay updated with current fashion trends.

The platform combines wardrobe organization, AI-based styling suggestions, and trend forecasting into a single elegant and user-friendly interface. It focuses on personalization, simplicity, and aesthetic design to enhance everyday fashion decisions.

---

## ❗ Problem Statement

In today’s fast-paced lifestyle, people often struggle with:

- Deciding what to wear daily  
- Underutilizing existing wardrobe items  
- Lack of outfit planning  
- Difficulty keeping up with fashion trends  
- Disorganized wardrobe management  

These issues lead to wasted time, repetitive outfits, and poor fashion choices.

---

## 💡 Solution

TrendAura provides a smart and stylish solution through:

- 👗 AI-based outfit recommendations  
- 📅 Smart wardrobe calendar & outfit planner  
- 📊 Trend prediction and style insights  
- 🧥 Digital wardrobe management system  
- 🎨 Personalized styling suggestions  
- 📱 Clean and aesthetic UI  

By integrating these features, TrendAura helps users make confident fashion decisions while maximizing their wardrobe usage.
